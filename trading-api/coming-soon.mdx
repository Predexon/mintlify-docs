## What's Coming

The Trading API will enable you to:

- **Place Orders** - Submit limit and market orders programmatically
- **Manage Positions** - Open, close, and modify your positions
- **Portfolio Management** - Automate your trading strategies
- **Real-time Execution** - Low-latency order execution
